Egg Chow Mein Recipe!

What is Biryani? I can't describe it with words. It is emotion. It is something that can can bring a smile on face in any situation.

Well if you ask google, it would say It is made with Indian spices, rice, meat (chicken, beef, goat, pork, lamb, prawn, or fish), vegetables or eggs. The word 'biryani' is derived from a Persian word, birian, which means fried before cooking. It is one of the most popular dishes, which has acquired a niche for itself in South Asian cuisine.

Recipe step by step

  1. slice cloves, onion and chilies on a cutting board
  2. slice green pepper, carrot and a spring onion and place these all on a plate and set aside.😀
  3. fill a medium-size sauce pan with water and place on medium-high heat until boiling.
  4. Once boiling, empty half a bag of chow mein noodles into the pan.
  5. Once the noodles are soft and tender, drain them in a strainer. Set the noodles aside.😁
  6. In a medium-bowl, beat 3 eggs and season with salt and pepper.
  7. Add a tablespoon of olive oil to a large pan. Place the pan on medium-heat.
  8. Add the beaten eggs to the pan and scramble. Once the eggs are cooked and scrambled, place them on a plate and set them aside.😄
  9. Using the same pan drizzle another tablespoon of oil. On medium-high heat, add the garlic, green chilies, and onion.
  10. Saute for about 1 minute then add the carrot, capsicum, and cabbage. Mix and saute for another 2-3 minutes.
  11. Add 1 tablespoon of soy sauce, ketchup, and chili sauce.😃
  12. Add 1/2 tablespoon of vinegar and mix it well. Add the boiled noodles and mix it.
  13. Once the noodles are mixed, add spring onion and scrambled eggs.
  14. After it is all mixed, dish and serve!😉
